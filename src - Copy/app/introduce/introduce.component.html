What is VoteChain?

Vote Chain is a Research and Development Project which is provided by AUT University and Bosch for senior students to apply and pratice what they have learned in 4 years. This project is about using Blockchain technology to build a voting application due to its improvement compare with Server-Client model: Robustness - Annonymous - Transparency.
By using Ethereum and Angular 5, our team has built an application for Bosch associates to create a poll and vote for their beliefs.
